COUNTRIES

View COVID-19 statistics such as total, active, recovered, and death cases from countries all over the world. The statistics data is retrieved and updated frequently by third-party providers.

NEWS

Stay up-to-date with the latest news about the COVID-19 situation in Singapore. The news data is retrieved from CNA for the latest information.

INFORMATION

Learn more about the COVID-19 virus with crowd-sourced data provided by contributers on Github. The compiled document provides details such as symptoms and general information about the virus.

SYMPTOM

The Educational COVID-19 Risk Assessment Tool by Infermedica provides a probability of COVID-19 risk based on answers provided for given questions. Although there is no gurantee in accuracy or diagnosis provided, it provides an educational and information insight to one's health.
